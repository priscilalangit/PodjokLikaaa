How To Make Likbap

Our Biotechnology product is Likbap. Likbap is a delicious Korean Kimbap made with fluffy white rice, rolled with fragrant toasted nori, and fresh fillings like cucumber, carrot, chicken, and egg roll.

We chose Likbap because it contains Kimchi, a biotechnology product involving biological fermentation. Microorganisms transform raw vegetables into more durable, nutritious food by breaking down sugars into organic acids. It's healthy, MSG-free, and perfect for teenagers!

Ingredients

  • 600g Chinese cabbage (3 medium)
  • Salt, 2 carrots, Spring onions
  • 1 cup water, 1 tbsp rice flour
  • 2 tsp sugar, 5 cloves garlic
  • 1/4 onion, 1 finger-length ginger
  • 5 tbsp fish sauce, 6 tbsp chili powder
  • Sesame oil, 10 sheets seaweed
  • 1 bowl rice, 30g chicken slice
  • 2 spoon ketchup

Tools

  • Spatula & Bowl
  • Table spoon & Tea spoon
  • Pan & Sushi roller
  • Knife

Instructions

1
Slice mustard greens lengthwise (vertically) and wash thoroughly.
2
Coat the surface and between leaves with salt evenly.
3
Let the coated greens sit for two hours.
4
Stir them every 30 minutes.
5
While waiting, grate the carrots lengthwise.
6
Chop the spring onions.
7
Combine rice flour and water in a pan until well mixed.
8
Bring the mixture to a boil.
9
Add sugar, stir until dissolved and thick. Turn off heat & set aside.
10
Finely chop the garlic, onion, and ginger.
11
Mix them into the rice flour mixture.
12
Add fish sauce and chili powder. Stir well.
13
Mix the carrots and spring onions into the mixture.
14
Thoroughly wash the mustard greens after 2 hours.
15
Rub the seasoning mixture evenly over the mustard greens.
16
Store in an airtight container at room temperature for two days.
17
Wait until water foams; the kimchi is ready!
18
Clean the sushi roller.
19
Place 1 sheet of seaweed on top of the roller.
20
Put about 4 tablespoons of rice.
21
Add kimchi, carrot, cucumber, and chicken on top.
22
Ensure it is neat, then roll it up with the sushi roller.
23
The Likbap is ready to eat!

How To Make Blup Blop Bag

Blup Blop Bag is a complete hair kit in one handy bag! It includes a mini comb, mirror, and clips. We made this because many students in Sanur need to pay more attention to their hair's neatness. Lightweight, unique, and stylish!

Ingredients

  • 1-2 Meter Batik Fabric (Lombok, NTB)
  • 1 Roll Of Red Thread
  • 2 Roll Of Macrame Rope

Tools

  • Sewing Machine & Thread Remover
  • Ruler & Scissors
  • Newspaper, Pencil & Sewing Chalk

Paper Template Steps

1
Sketch a creative bag design onto newspaper using a pencil and ruler.
2
Cut out the final shape carefully to create the master template.

Final Construction

1
Trace the Batik fabric using sewing chalk according to the template.
2
Cut the fabric pieces precisely following the chalk lines.
3
Sew all pieces together using the sewing machine and red thread.
4
Attach macrame rope to the top for a sturdy and stylish handle.
5
Remove excess or loose threads with a thread remover.
6
The Blup Blop Bag is ready to keep your hair accessories organized!
